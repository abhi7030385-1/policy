Welcome to Testing App (operated by PickBazaar). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

This policy is designed to comply with global data protection standards and the strict privacy guidelines set forth by the Google Play Store (2026 Guidelines). By using our App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Data Collected

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the App, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products, or otherwise when you contact us. We collect the following data:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number (Optional)
  • Delivery Address (Manually entered by you)
  • Profile Photo (if uploaded)
  • Order History
  • Device Information
  • Firebase Notification Token (FCM)

2. Data Not Collected

We respect your privacy and strictly do NOT collect or request access to the following:

  • Live Location (GPS Data)
  • Audio Recordings
  • Contacts
  • SMS Messages
  • Call Logs

3. Device Permissions We Request

To provide core functionalities, our App may request the following permissions on your device:

  • Photos and Media (Storage): Used to allow users to select and upload profile pictures, product images, and other media files from their device gallery.
  • Internet / Network State: Required to connect the App to our secure cloud servers to fetch products and sync your cart.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To Fulfill and Manage Your Orders: We use your name, phone number, and manually entered address to process your orders and facilitate deliveries.
  • To Facilitate Account Creation and Logon: Your email or phone number is used to securely authenticate your identity.
  • To Send Notifications: We use FCM tokens to send you push notifications regarding your order status.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We only share information with trusted third-party service providers to facilitate our services securely:

  • Google Firebase: We use Google Firebase services for authentication, database management, cloud messaging (notifications), and application functionality.
  • Cloudflare: Cloudflare may be used to securely store, process, and deliver user-uploaded media content such as images and videos.
  • Payment Processors: We do not store your credit card details. In the future, we may integrate secure payment gateways such as Razorpay to process online payments securely. Once integrated, Razorpay will handle transaction data according to their strict security policies.
  • Sellers & Delivery Agents: When you place an order, we share your name and delivery address with the specific seller and delivery partner fulfilling your order.
