Privacy Policy (Seller Partner)

Effective Date: June 05, 2026

Welcome to the PickBazaar Seller application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data of our merchant partners and store owners in compliance with Google Play Store 2026 guidelines.

1. Data Collected

We collect the following data to enable you to operate your store on our platform:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Store Information (Name, Description, Store Logos/Banners)
  • Business Location (GPS Coordinates and Address)
  • Legal & Identity Data (Tax IDs, Business registration)
  • Financial Data (Bank account details for payouts)
  • Product Media (Images, Videos, Descriptions)
  • Device Information & Firebase Notification Token (FCM)

2. Data Not Collected

We do NOT collect or request access to the following:

  • Audio Recordings (Background)
  • Contacts
  • SMS Messages
  • Call Logs

3. Device Permissions We Request

  • Photos and Media (Storage): Used to allow users to select and upload profile pictures, product images, and other media files from their device gallery.
  • Location: To accurately verify and pin your store's physical location so delivery agents and customers can find you.
  • Camera: Used strictly when you choose to take live photos of your products or record promotional video reels.
  • Internet: To sync your inventory and receive real-time orders.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Your store name, public location, and products are visible to customers on the PickBazaar app.
  • We share your store's physical location with Delivery Agents to facilitate order pickups.
  • Your financial data is securely used to process your sales payouts and settlements.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

  • Google Firebase: We use Google Firebase services for authentication, database management, cloud messaging (notifications), and application functionality.
  • Cloudflare: Cloudflare may be used to securely store, process, and deliver user-uploaded media content such as images and videos.
  • Payment Processors: We use certified payment gateways to process your payouts. In the future, we may integrate Razorpay to process online transactions securely.

6. Account and Data Deletion

Users may request account deletion from within the application or by emailing abhi030385@gmail.com from their registered email address.

Note: Due to financial regulations and tax compliance, certain transaction records must be retained for a legally mandated period even after account deletion.
