Privacy Policy (Delivery Partner)

Effective Date: June 05, 2026

Welcome to the PickBazaar Delivery application. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the personal data of our delivery partners ("Riders") in compliance with Google Play Store 2026 guidelines.

1. Background Location Data (Prominent Disclosure)

PickBazaar Delivery collects location data to enable order assignments and provide live order tracking for customers and sellers even when the app is closed or not in use.

2. Data Collected

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Live Location (GPS Data - Foreground & Background)
  • Profile Photo (if uploaded)
  • Identity & Vehicle Documents (License, RC, etc.)
  • Financial Data (Bank account details for earnings payouts)
  • Order History
  • Device Information & Firebase Notification Token (FCM)

3. Data Not Collected

We strictly do NOT collect or request access to the following:

  • Audio Recordings
  • Contacts
  • SMS Messages
  • Call Logs

4. Device Permissions We Request

  • Location (Background/Foreground): Essential for order dispatching and live customer tracking as detailed above.
  • Photos and Media: Used to allow users to select and upload profile pictures, product images, and other media files from their device gallery.
  • Internet: To connect the App to our secure cloud servers.
  • Camera: To take your profile picture, capture photos of identity documents during onboarding, and take "Proof of Delivery" photos.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity and legal eligibility to work as a delivery partner.
  • To dynamically calculate your delivery distances, routes, and corresponding payouts.
  • To share your name, phone number, and live location with the customer and seller only during an active delivery task.

6. Third-Party Service Providers

  • Google Firebase: We use Google Firebase services for authentication, database management, cloud messaging (notifications), and application functionality.
  • Cloudflare: Cloudflare may be used to securely store, process, and deliver user-uploaded media content such as images and videos.
  • Payment Processors: We use certified payment gateways to process your payouts. In the future, we may integrate Razorpay to process online transactions securely.

7. Account and Data Deletion

Users may request account deletion from within the application or by emailing abhi030385@gmail.com from their registered email address.
